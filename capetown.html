CapeTown

Itinerary

Day 1:

  • Morning: Hike or take a cable car to Table Mountain.
  • Afternoon: Visit the V&A Waterfront for lunch and shopping.
  • Evening: Sunset at Signal Hill or Camps Bay Beach.

Day 2:

  • Morning: Take a day trip to Cape Point via the scenic Chapman’s Peak Drive.
  • Afternoon: Stop by Boulders Beach to see the penguins.
  • Evening: Return to Cape Town and explore Long Street for nightlife.

Day 3:

  • Morning: Visit Robben Island for a historical tour.
  • Afternoon: Explore Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens.
  • Evening: Enjoy a traditional South African braai (BBQ).
